About Kinsar Intelligence

Founder-led delivery with technical depth and operational judgment

Kinsar Intelligence LLP is an AI consulting and engineering firm focused on building practical systems on serious data foundations. We help clients move from unclear opportunity to production-ready intelligence that fits real operating workflows — covering strategy, engineering, and adoption without handing off context between phases.

What Kinsar optimises for

Useful systems over novelty demos. Every engagement is anchored to measurable bottlenecks, real workflows, and decisions that need better support. We avoid POC theatre.

How Kinsar works

Advisory plus implementation: framing the use case, shaping the data model, building the intelligence layer, and operationalising the system for team adoption — all founder-led.

Engagement types

AI strategy and opportunity mapping, data platform engineering, RAG and knowledge layers, forecasting and decision support, ML governance, and AEM or SFMC digital delivery.

Typical Kinsar engagements span AI strategy sessions, data-intensive model systems, retrieval-augmented generation pipelines, forecasting and decision-support tools, and front-end experience delivery across AEM and SFMC ecosystems.
